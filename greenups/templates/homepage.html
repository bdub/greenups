{% block page_title %}About Greenups{% endblock page_title %}

Greenups is a local Sydney-based not-for-profit community group.

We put on 'think and link' networking events to raise awareness and promote creative solutions to environmental and sustainability issues, attracting a mix of the professional, the passionate and the curious.

Each month we explore a topic of broad general interest through short talks, shared ideas and creative conversations.

At our next Event we're looking at "Plastic" as part of Plastic Free July.

How's it work

The Greenups format is causal mingling/networking in a bar setting usually with a formal proceedings of a welcome and typically 3 lightning talks followed by a Q&A with the audience. The talks are usually done in an hour, with the conversations continuing at the bar or over some food. It usually wraps up around 9 or 10.

Greenups talks are only 5 mins long, typically ( but it's amazing what you can say in 5 minutes if required!)

When's it on?

Greenups is on the "First Tuesday of the Month", we try to keep that consistent so you can just put it in your calander and easily remember. with differnt themes venues nad locations it's nice to keep something consistent. Having said that we have sometimes moved the date in order to coincide with something, but that's definitly the exceptin.

Typically, Greenups starts at 6pm, with the talks at 7pm running for about an hour. after the talks most guests stick aroung for a chat and more drinks and food. It's all very casal.

Get Involved

Greenups is run by a small group of passionate individuals who enjoy creating a forum for idea sharing, connecting, discovering and creating solutions to make our lives more sustainable.

The core team is always looking of help, big or small, there's plenty to, so if your keen to get involved please do get in touch - no experience required :)

Suggest a Theme

Do you have a great idea for a theme that you'd like to see Greenups focus on? Is it an area that you have some in-depth knowledge on, or just a topic that you're interested in and would like to know more about? We're open to anything broadly related to ecologically conscious living, if you've got an idea, give us a shout, the likely-hood is if you're interested in it, we'll be too.

Suggest a Speaker

Do you know someone amazing who really deserves to get their story out to the Greenups crowd, please let us know we're always looking out for speakers.

Host an Event

We love colaborating. Whether you're an individual or an orgainisation, if you'd like to work with Greenups to host an Event please get in touch.

Know a great Venue?

Do you run a venue? Or perhaps you're a super "in the know" barfly up on all the great watering holes, either way if you know of somewhere that would be great to hold a GreenUps then please let us know. we love discovering and supporing independent venues that 'get sustaiability'.
